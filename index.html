1. Introduction

Welcome to Advisor Desk. This application is designed to help customer care advisors track their work performance, manage daily entries, and calculate earnings efficiently. 2. Information We Handle

Data You Provide

Advisor Desk processes information that you voluntarily enter into the app. This includes:

  • Daily Work Logs: Login hours, minutes, seconds, and call counts.
  • Performance Metrics: CSAT (T2, B2, N counts) and CQ (Call Quality) scores.
  • Goal Settings: Your personal monthly targets for calls and hours.
  • Salary Parameters: Customizable settings for salary calculation (e.g., rate per call, bonus amounts).

Data We DO NOT Collect

We want to be crystal clear. Advisor Desk DOES NOT collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). There are:

  • No user accounts or sign-ups.
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information collected.
  • No server-side storage. Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it.

3. Data Storage and Security

All the information you enter into Advisor Desk is stored in a local SQFlite database directly on your device's internal storage. This means:

  • You have full control: Your data is yours alone. We cannot access, view, or manage it.
  • Offline Functionality: The app works perfectly without an internet connection.
  • Security is in your hands: The security of your data depends on the security of your device (e.g., screen lock, encryption).
  • Backup & Restore: You are responsible for backing up your data. The app provides a feature to export the database for backup purposes.

4. App Permissions

To provide its full functionality, Advisor Desk may request the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: This permission is required to save generated performance reports (in PDF or Excel format) to your device's storage and to enable the database backup/restore feature.
  • Device Information: When you voluntarily send feedback to the developer, the app may attach basic, non-personal device information (like OS version and app version) to the email. This helps us debug issues more effectively. This information is only sent if you initiate the feedback process.

5. How Your Data Is Used

The data you provide is used solely for the app's core features, which operate locally on your device:

  • To calculate your monthly performance summaries.
  • To automatically compute your estimated salary, including bonuses and deductions.
  • To visualize your progress against your monthly goals.
  • To generate detailed PDF and Excel reports for your personal records.

6. Third-Party Services

Advisor Desk does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for professionals and is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
